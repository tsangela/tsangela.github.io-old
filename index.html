Hungry Cat (Java)

A simple snake-like game featuring a very hungry cat. Instructions: You are playing as a cat! Use the arrow keys to move around, but avoid hitting walls. Different foods have different nutritional values. The more food you eat, the faster you run! Avoid hitting walls. During gameplay, press the space bar to pause the game.
